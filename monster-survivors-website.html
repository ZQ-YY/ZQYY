About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Your objective is to survive as long as possible while battling increasingly challenging enemies.

Unlock powerful upgrades and abilities as you progress, and strategize your build to enhance your chances of survival!

The game features:

  • Endless waves of unique monsters
  • Character progression and upgrades
  • Multiple weapon types and special abilities
  • Beautiful visuals and engaging gameplay
  • Challenging mechanics that test your skills

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.

Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Press Space to use special abilities once unlocked.

Tips & Strategies

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters.

Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up.

Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle.
