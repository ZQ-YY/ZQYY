About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of terrifying monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters to earn experience and gold.

As you progress, you'll unlock powerful upgrades and abilities that help you combat increasingly difficult enemies. The game features intuitive controls, stunning visuals, and addictive gameplay that will keep you coming back for more.

Perfect for quick gaming sessions or long playthroughs, Monster Survivors offers endless replayability with its randomly generated enemy waves and multiple character classes to master.

Key Features

Fast-Paced Action

Intense battles with waves of monsters that test your reflexes and strategy.

Upgrade System

Unlock powerful skills and upgrades as you progress through the game.

Multiple Classes

Choose from different character classes, each with unique abilities and playstyles.

How to Play Monster Survivors

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield
  2. Left-click or tap to attack nearby monsters
  3. Collect experience orbs to level up when the meter fills
  4. Choose upgrades carefully when you level up to build your perfect survivor
  5. Survive as long as possible and try to beat your high score!

Tips & Strategies

Early Game Focus

In the first few minutes, prioritize movement speed and area-of-effect upgrades to handle the increasing number of monsters.

Balanced Builds

Don't focus on just one type of upgrade. A balanced character with good damage, defense, and mobility will survive longer.

Kiting Technique

Learn to "kite" enemies by moving away while attacking. This is especially important against tougher monsters.
