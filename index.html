About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where players must defend themselves against relentless waves of terrifying monsters.

Set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by creatures of the night, your goal is simple: survive as long as possible. With each passing minute, the monsters grow stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategy to the limit.

Originally developed by a team of passionate indie developers, Monster Survivors has grown into one of the most popular survival games online, with millions of players worldwide.
